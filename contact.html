F.A.Qs

Will everything be taken away after the job is done?

Yes, all tree cuttings and wastage and equipment will be taken away from your property at the end of the job. We aim to carry out the job in hand with great quality and provide a professional service.

What will happen to the waste cuttings and stump chippings?

All cuttings and biodegradable waste will be removed, processed and recycled efficiently and appropriately. However, some customers may wish to keep these biodegradable waste products for compost or other reusable purposes.

Will my neighbours be affected?

In certain situations the job may require us to have access to your neighbour's property to complete the maintenance. In some circumstances you will need to inform your neighbours and get permission for us to access their property in order to complete the job in hand.

How do I know if a tree or hedge row needs maintenance?

Trees and hedges need maintained for several reasons, perhaps they are overgrown and could pose a danger to your property or someone else's, or they may be unhealthy and need professional tree care and maintenance.
