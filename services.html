Tree Maintenance

To maintain the health of your trees and safety of your environment, it is important to carry out good quality tree maintenance where required. Trees need essential maintenance for healthy growth and to ensure they are not a danger to people or their surroundings.

Tree Felling

Sometimes a tree needs to be cut down or felled. This could be because it is in the way of a road or a building, it is too old or maybe it has a disease which could spread to other trees. Tree felling is potantially dangerous and must be carried out by a professional arborist or tree surgeon.

Tree Planting

Trees are an attractive feature in any environment. Planting trees is not only great for the environment, it is also an excellent addition to your garden or property.

Hedge Cutting

Just like a lawn or a head of hair, hedges need trimmed and maintained in order to retain good aesthetics and maintain healthy growth.

Stump Grinding
